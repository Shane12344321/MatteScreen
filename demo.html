Make your screen feel like paper.

Soft, paper-like textures that settle over your whole screen, taking the glare and harsh contrast out of everything you do.

This page is a standalone preview of the texture engine used by the MatteScreen desktop app. Toggle the effect with the panel in the corner and switch between textures. In the real app, this same layer floats above every window on every monitor, fully click-through.

Not a blue light filter.

Night Shift and f.lux change color temperature. MatteScreen changes screen texture — it diffuses highlights and attenuates contrast, like a physical matte screen protector, but in software.

Procedural grain

The texture is generated with SVG feTurbulence, so it stays crisp at any resolution and ships with zero image assets.
